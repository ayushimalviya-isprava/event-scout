## Objective (the filter)
Find US events (major cities) worth **NDS's** time — founder of Isprava (luxury real
estate, ~₹800 Cr), Mumbai-based, AI-first. Goal: meet **peers and people to learn from**
in (1) real estate / proptech, (2) AI, (3) high-growth tech, (4) capital / family offices.
**5 exceptional events a year, not 30 average ones.** Window: **Nov 2026 → Nov 2027**.

An event qualifies only if it clears ≥3 of: peer density (principals attend in person) ·
access (dinners/small summits/curated 1:1s, not a 2,000-seat stage) · strategic adjacency ·
signal (track record + nameable attendees) · asymmetry (people he couldn't reach cold).
Disqualify: pay-to-play, vendor/sales-heavy, mostly analysts/students/junior, pure
education available from a podcast, unverifiable, date/venue unconfirmable.
