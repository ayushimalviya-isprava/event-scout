## OBJECTIVE (the filter)

Find **US events (major cities)** worth **NDS's** time — founder of **Isprava** (luxury real
estate, ~₹800 Cr), Mumbai-based, **AI-first**. Window: **Nov 2026 → Nov 2027**.
Principle: **5 exceptional events a year, not 30 average ones.** Default to SKIP.

### What NDS is there to do (his posture — drives tiering)
1. **See how US real-estate principals deploy AI & tech, and meet those operators.**
   ← this is the center of gravity. The intersection of *real estate × AI/tech* outranks
   generic AI and generic real estate.
2. **Peer relationships & learning** — founders/CEOs at or above his level, in person.
3. **Allocate / co-invest** — he deploys capital as an investor. → allocator/family-office
   rooms (iConnections, PERE, family-office co-invest) move **up** a tier, not down.
4. **US expansion for Isprava** — brand, partners, buyers. → NRI/HNI luxury-buyer rooms,
   US–India corridor, branded residences, family-office real estate are **in scope**.
5. **NOT raising capital** — deprioritize pure LP-raise / fund-marketing forums.

### The bar — qualify only if ≥3 of:
- **Peer density** — principals attend in person (not junior staff).
- **Access** — dinners / small summits / curated 1:1s, not a 2,000-seat stage.
- **Strategic adjacency** — real estate × AI/tech, capital (allocating), or US expansion.
- **Signal** — track record + nameable attendees.
- **Asymmetry** — people he couldn't reach with a cold email.

### Disqualify immediately:
Pay-to-play · vendor/sales-heavy · mostly analysts/students/junior · pure education a podcast
covers · unverifiable · date/venue unconfirmable · pure capital-raise marketing.

### 9. Side-events  — weight: MED (high ROI)

### 10. LinkedIn & X — public-post discovery  — weight: MED (variable signal)
Search indexes public LinkedIn/X posts; mine them for event announcements, side-event/dinner
invites, and "applications open" calls that never hit a normal event site.
**Public posts only — NO logged-in feed scraping (their ToS / paid API forbid it).** Signal is
noisy and often non-US; treat hits as leads and verify on the official site before listing.
- Scope searches to domains `linkedin.com` and `x.com` (use the search tool's domain filter).
- Patterns: `site:linkedin.com/posts "AI in real estate" 2027` · `site:x.com "founder dinner" <city>`
  · `site:linkedin.com proptech summit 2027` · `site:x.com "applications open" AI summit 2027`
  · `site:linkedin.com TiE OR USISPF event 2027`
- Accounts worth monitoring (they announce events/side-events): proptech VCs (MetaProp,
  Camber Creek, Fifth Wall), frontier-AI founders & labs, Cerebral Valley / Newcomer,
  TiE / USISPF handles, major real-estate-tech operators. Add specific handles as found.
