# Network-gated tier — "target, don't book"

The highest-value rooms aren't ticketed. You can't buy in; you get in via membership,
nomination, or relationship. Below are the **verified path-ins** (Pass 2, 2026-06-22).
These are NOT on the calendar UI (they're memberships/invite circuits, not dated events).

## TIGER 21 — ACTIONABLE for NDS in India ★
Peer network of UHNW individuals; groups of 15 meet monthly, present their full financial
lives for peer review. **India groups are now live** — the realistic door.
- **Bar:** the "5Cs" — **≥ $20M USD** verifiable investable assets/net worth; background check
  + interview; commitment to monthly meetings. [Confirmed — tiger21.com]
- **India chairs:** Mumbai 01 — Pritam Doshi (Quark Solar); Mumbai 02 — Manoj Gursahani;
  Bengaluru 01 — Vikaash Khdloya (Boundless Capital). [Confirmed — The Wire, Oct 2025]
- **Path in:** submit a membership inquiry → a Member Development Director makes contact →
  in-person meeting with a Group Chair. ~2-month process.
  https://tiger21.com/membership-inquiry/
- **Verdict:** the most reachable elite peer room for NDS — it's in his city, and he clears
  the bar. Worth a direct inquiry.

## R360 — high bar, nomination-only
UHNW family & peer network; whole families welcome.
- **Bar:** **≥ $100M net worth**; 3-year program **~$180,000**; nomination by an existing
  member + background checks + references. Capped 500 US / 500 global; highly selective
  (has rejected 70+ applicants incl. billionaires). [Confirmed — Spear's, Robb Report, r360global.com]
- **Path in:** needs an **existing-member nomination** — not a cold application. Identify a
  member in his network first. US chapters (incl. a new DC metro chapter).
- **Verdict:** target only if a warm nominator exists; otherwise lower priority than TIGER 21.

## Allen & Co Sun Valley — invite-only, no door to buy
Media/tech/finance summit, ~July, Sun Valley ID. No public site, no application. [Unconfirmed dates]
- **Path in:** there is no application. You get invited by being a CEO Allen & Co wants in the
  room, usually surfaced via top-tier banker / a16z / Sequoia relationships.
- **Verdict:** long game. Track, don't book. Build the banker/VC relationships that put him on
  the radar; revisit in a few years.
